Heart of Afghanistan

10 Days of Authentic Cultural Immersion

€ 3,499 per person

10 days, double occupancy

Tour Overview

Embark on a transformative journey through Afghanistan's cultural heartlands. This 10-day adventure takes you from the bustling streets of Kabul to the ancient wonders of Herat and the spiritual center of Mazar-i-Sharif.

Duration

10 Days

Group Size

4-12 People

Difficulty

Moderate

Accommodation

Local Homes

Day-by-Day Itinerary

1

Day 1: Arrival in Kabul

Welcome to Afghanistan! Transfer to your accommodation and settle in with a traditional Afghan dinner.

2

Day 2: Kabul Exploration

Visit the National Museum, Babur Gardens, and Shah-e Doh Shamshira Mosque. Experience Kabul's rich history.

3

Day 3: Kabul Culture

Explore the Darul Aman Palace and Kabul City Center. Meet local artisans and learn about Afghan crafts.

4

Day 4: Flight to Herat

Morning flight to Herat. Visit the Friday Mosque and explore the ancient city's architecture.

5

Day 5: Herat Heritage

Discover the Mausoleum of Queen Gauhar Shad and the Five-Minaret Complex. Experience Herat's artistic legacy.

6

Day 6: Drive to Mazar-i-Sharif

Scenic drive through Afghanistan's landscapes to Mazar-i-Sharif, the country's fourth-largest city.

7

Day 7: Blue Mosque & Shrines

Visit the stunning Blue Mosque and the Shrine of Ali. Explore ancient Balkh, once a center of learning.

8

Day 8: Return to Kabul

Flight back to Kabul. Free time for shopping and reflection on your journey.

9

Day 9: Kabul Farewell

Final explorations and cultural experiences in Kabul. Farewell dinner with your guides.

10

Day 10: Departure

Transfer to the airport for your international flight home.
